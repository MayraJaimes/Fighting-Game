Fighting Game

Instructions:

  1. For this game you first have to pick a heroe.
  2. Then pick the villain you want to fight first.
  3. If you defeat the first villian you will go on to fight the next villain you pick

The goal is the game is to reduce the villains health points to 0 or below. If at any point your character's health points get to 0 or below you lose the game. If you defeat all the villains you win!
